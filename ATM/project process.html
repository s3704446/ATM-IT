Working as a group for the last 12 weeks, some of the things that we have learned about group projects are: Organization: In each group meeting everyone is very active organization and participation. We actively discuss and solve problems no matter in class or after class. The group members can give each other better Suggestions Time management: We attach great importance to the time management of assignments. We put the things to Trello that should be done every week carefully so that it is very clear. The communication between everyone in our group is efficient. Everyone can come up with good ideas. From your experience in this project, what is the most important aspect of organizing a project of this nature? Is there anything that you would do differently if you were to start again? From our group's experience of this project, time management is very important for us. We should try to finish the task one week before the deadline, so that we still have time to discuss and revise. We worked ahead of time through Trello's schedule, and we tried our best to finish the work a few days before the scheduled time, which was also convenient for the weekly teacher meeting. What advice would you give to a group about to embark on a similar project?
